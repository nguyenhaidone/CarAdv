Take your new MINI Countryman off-road without going out of style. The optional MINI ALL4 Exterior Optic pack includes black high-gloss bumper elements, silver underbody protection sills and roof rails with silver side sills.

SEMI-COMMAND SEATING POSITION.
Take charge of the road. The MINI Countryman’s elevated seats give you a clear overview of surrounding traffic and terrain ahead when you’ve swapped the road for off-road.

HANDS FREE OPENING.
If your hands are full or you don’t want to put what you’re carrying down on the ground, the optional hands-free Easy Opener* function can give you a helpful hand while loading your luggage into your MINI Countryman’s spacious boot. Simply kick your foot under the rear sill and the boot door opens automatically. A second kick and it closes again. Just imagine how helpful that would be if you have kids, pets or bulky shopping in your arms. *Available with the Comfort Access system *Available with the Comfort Access system.
